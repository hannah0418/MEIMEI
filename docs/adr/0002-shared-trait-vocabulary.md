# Traits are a small shared vocabulary, not the Personas' own adjectives

The adjectives written on the eight Personas gave us 21 distinct traits across 24 slots — 18 of them belonging to exactly one Persona, and several (Precise / Sharp / Detail-Oriented / Methodical) being one idea in four costumes. Scoring those directly would make each Trait a rename of a Persona, reachable only by its own questions, and would split a meticulous respondent's score four ways so they matched nobody. We consolidated to ten Traits, each feeding two or more Personas, so that twelve questions can discriminate eight outcomes.

## Consequences

The Personas' original adjectives survive as **Descriptors** — printed on the reveal card, never scored. A Trait→Persona weight table is now required, and it is the artifact that decides whether a Persona is reachable at all.
