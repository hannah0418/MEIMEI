# MEI MEI opens on its landing experience before the quiz

The kiosk-only rewrite removed the original Home, How to Play, and Personas screens and opened directly on the boards. MEI MEI restores those three pre-quiz screens so the fair has an introduction and students can understand the two Rounds and Persona line-up before entering a Name; after a completed Response, the Reveal still returns to the live boards so the recruiting loop from ADR-0008 remains intact.
